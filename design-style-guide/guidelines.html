Guidelines

Working on any part of this brand, whether that is on the website or in the facilities certain things are important to know. Here we present the personality we want to convey - what we want people to think of when they hear the name Snow Fun!

It is also important to know to whom we speak. Who are those we want to reach? Who are the people skiing down our tracks or icecycling on nearby icy waters? And last, but equally important is HOW we are talking to those people. What kind of words do we use? What kind of tone are we showing?

Getting to know these aspects and implementing them in our being as we represent this company, plays a big part in becoming an ambassadeur for the outdoor fun this club want to bring out to the people.

Brand Personality

Keyword: EXCITEMENT!

We want to be fun and easy! It should be convenient to get here, and all facilites are met, with dining, drinking, and having fun together with friends. We want to present all kinds of winter sports to our visitor, to get they excited and happy to be here.

Target Audience

Keywords: Young adults and adults

This is a winter sports club for adult audience. This is a place where young adults and adults can come to have childishlike fun in the snow. There are several other family-places, this one is for adults from 18 years and up.

Voice and Tone

Keywords: Friendly and Helpful

The way we speak to our audience is the way we speak to our best friend when they are hung over - friendly and helpful, but if they need guidance, we will provide that in an amicable way.
