Layout

Choosing the right layout is important to keep the consistency of the website throughout different pages.

Grids and Columns

We use a 12-column grid for desktop size, and a 4-column grid for mobile.

Different pages uses this grid differently. Be sure to understand what kind of page you are building when choosing variant of this grid.

Desktop 12-grid

Mobile 4-grid

Home Page

Hierarchical Grid

Here we have 1 main content area, with 2 smaller on the right side.The main area takes up 6 column in width, and is as long as the other two together. This main area is the most important part of the page, and the cta should be clear here.

Content on the right side is news and coming events.

Below the fold, we use a Modular Grid, where we divide this 12 columns up in 4-column width columns, 3 columns in total for width, 2 or more rows, for showing interesting winter sports and activities.
