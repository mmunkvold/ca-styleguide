Design Style Guide

Here you'll find everything you need for making Snow Fun!'s website look great!

For whom is this guide created?

This guide is created to help all those who work on this site, developers, graphic designers, ui and ux designers...m m. Everyone that is going to do something on this site, will use this guide.

If you find that something in here is wrong or there is missing information, be sure to tell your team designer. This guide is still under work.

How to use it

In the menu you'll find different topics. Under each one of them, you'll find specifications on the element/component for which you are to work with. These specifications help you choose right colors, placement, size etc.

Example:

Let's say you're adding a form to a page, click "Forms" in the menu and follow the directions given.

If you are unsure of anything regarding this guide or how to use it, please ask your colleagues or the main designer on your team.
